Requirements

The ability to deploy hundreds of 3C sensors in a well and make it cost effective comes with some limitations:

Wells

  • Wells should be cased, with at least 3.8 inch internal diameter casing (steel, PVC or fibreglass);
  • Wells can not be under pressure;
  • Wells should be vertical or near vertical;

The array

  • Temperature limited to < 75 degrees celsius for digital sensors;
  • Maximum sample rate is 1ms (375Hz anti alias);
  • Currently the maximum depth is 720m with a 3C sensor every 2m

 

Please contact us to discuss your exploration needs with us. If our existing system does not quite meet your requirements, it may be possible to adapt the tool to meet your needs.

HDVSP Features at a Glance

  • 3-component
  • MEMS sensors
  • 1 or 2 metre sensor interval
  • 100's of sensors per well
  • Up to 720m (current maximum)
  • 1ms sample rate
  • 2D, 3D & 4D
  • Multi-well VSPs

Standalone or simultaneous recording with surface seismic
